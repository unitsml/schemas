Element Float64ConversionFrom

Element for providing factors for a conversion equation from another unit; y = d + ((b / c) (x + a)). Note: The related "conversion to" equation is a simple inversion of the above equation; i.e., x = ((c / b) (y - d)) - a.

Element SpecialConversionFrom

Attribute multiplicand

Attribute divisor

Attribute divisorDigits

Attribute finalAddend

Attribute finalAddendDecimalPlace

Indicates the position of the least significant digit (in decimal) of the finalAddend; the position of this digit is given by ten to additive inverse of this number.
